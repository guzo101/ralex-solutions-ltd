How ENTSA Daily Savings Works

Each ENTSA slot is a daily commitment that helps you build long-term financial discipline:

  • K100 Slot - Save K100 per day
  • K200 Slot - Save K200 per day
  • K300 Slot - Save K300 per day
  • Choose one slot or multiple slots
  • The more slots you take, the more you save
  • Build a daily saving habit with simple structure
  • Prepare for rent and school fees with confidence
  • Create emergency readiness through discipline
  • Support small business cash-flow stability
  • Grow savings progressively through consistency
  • Suitable for market traders and bus drivers
  • Suitable for shop workers and salaried employees
